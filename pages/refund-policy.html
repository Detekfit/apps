Refund Policy

Clear and fair refunds for ThinkInk subscriptions and purchases.

Overview

We want you to enjoy ThinkInk. Once a purchase is made, it’s final, and no refunds are applicable. This policy explains the conditions surrounding digital product purchases, subscriptions, and how to handle issues in case of failed transactions.


Purchase Policy

  • Once a digital product or subscription is successfully purchased, it is final. All tokens, whether for subscriptions or in-app purchases, must be used within the app ThinkInk and cannot be refunded.
  • A subscription can only be used on the device where it was purchased. If you purchase a monthly subscription, you will only be able to access the benefits from the account or device where the purchase was made.

Failed Purchase

  • If your purchase fails, please contact us at support@detekfit.com. Once the failure is verified, the digital goods will be directly added to your account.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • All purchases, once successfully completed, are final and cannot be refunded.
  • Any issues with purchases must be reported immediately, and we will work to resolve the problem as soon as possible.

How to Request Support for Failed Purchases

  1. If your purchase failed, email us at support@detekfit.com.
  2. Include your account email, order ID, and a short description of the issue.
  3. We aim to respond within 2 business days and verify the failed transaction.

Subscriptions Managed by App Stores

If you purchased via the Apple App Store or Google Play, their policies apply. Refunds for such purchases must be requested directly through the respective app store support channels.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@detekfit.com.

This policy is for general guidance. Local laws may provide additional rights. We may update this page as our service evolves.
