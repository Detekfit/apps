Refund Policy

Clear and fair refunds for ThinkInk subscriptions and purchases.

Overview

We want you to enjoy ThinkInk. If something isn’t working for you, please contact us and we’ll help. This policy explains when refunds may apply and how to request them.

Eligibility window

  • New subscriptions: full refund within 14 days of initial purchase if you haven’t used premium features extensively.
  • Renewals: contact us within 7 days of the renewal charge for a review.
  • One‑time purchases: 14 days from purchase date if the item is defective or not as described.

Non‑refundable cases

  • Abuse or excessive usage during the trial/early access period.
  • Requests made significantly after the stated windows.
  • Charges from third‑party platforms or marketplaces—please contact the platform directly.

How refunds are issued

  • Refunds go back to the original payment method when possible.
  • Processing time depends on your bank/payment provider (typically 5–10 business days).
  • If a refund isn’t possible to the original method, we may offer store credit as an alternative.

How to request a refund

  1. Email us at support@yourdomain.com.
  2. Include your account email, order ID, and a short description of the issue.
  3. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Subscriptions managed by app stores

If you purchased via Apple App Store or Google Play, their policies apply and refunds must be requested through the respective store account support.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@yourdomain.com.

This policy is for general guidance. Local laws may provide additional rights. We may update this page as our service evolves.
