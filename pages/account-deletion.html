Request account deletion

You can request the deletion of your ThinkInk account and associated data. Follow the steps below.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Submit the deletion request form below (Google Forms).
  2. Check your email and confirm the request (we verify ownership).
  3. We will process deletion within 7 days and notify you when complete.

If you can’t access your email or have special circumstances, use the fallback form or contact support.

What is deleted?

We delete your account profile, saved prompts, local history (if synced), and any associated identifiers.

Note: Data stored only on your device (local-first history) is under your control. You can clear it anytime in-app.

Need help?

If you run into issues, reach us and we’ll assist promptly.
