Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Overview

ThinkInk is privacy‑minded. We aim for a local‑first experience where possible.

Data We Collect

  • App telemetry (crash logs, basic usage metrics) to improve performance.
  • Optional account data if you sign up for Pro or Teams.
  • Locally stored prompt history — you control it and can clear it anytime.

How We Use Data

  • To operate and improve the app experience and reliability.
  • To provide Pro/Teams features and support.
  • To communicate important updates (you can opt out of marketing emails).

Retention & Control

You can clear local history at any time in the app. Account data is retained while your subscription is active and as required by law.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@thinkink.app.
