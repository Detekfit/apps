Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Overview

ThinkInk is privacy‑minded. We aim for a local‑first experience where possible.

- Optional account data for Pro/Teams.
- Local prompt history you can clear anytime.

How We Use Data

- Operate and improve the app.
- Provide Pro/Teams features and support.
- Communicate important updates (opt out of marketing).

Retention & Control

Clear local history anytime. Account data retained while active and as required by law.
