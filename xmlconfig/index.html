What is XML Configs?

XML Configs: Camera Presets is the ultimate tool for mobile photography enthusiasts who want to unlock the full potential of their modded camera applications.

Our app provides a comprehensive library of XML configuration files that allow you to customize and optimize various camera parameters. Whether you're using Google Camera ports like GCam, LMC, BSG, or SGcam, our configurations help you achieve professional-quality photos on your Android device.

The app features an intuitive interface that makes it easy to browse, preview, and download configurations specifically tailored for your device model. With over 1 million downloads and thousands of available configs, XML Configs has become the go-to resource for the mobile photography community.
